About us

Our quest for engineering excellence began in 1988, with the establishment of our first manufacturing plant at Isnapur, Hyderabad. Subsequently, we embarked on a series of strategic acquisitions and expansion plans.

We are a multi-location, multi-product company having a significant presence in sectors like Infrastructure, Automobiles, Power, General Engineering, Building & Construction among others. Driven by our guiding philosophy, we have successfully established our identity as a Powerhouse of Engineering Excellence.
